Christoph Schwering

I'm a postdoc at UNSW Sydney. Before that, I did a PhD at RWTH Aachen in Gerhard Lakemeyer's group.

My research concerns commonsense reasoning, a branch of artifial intelligence. I'm particularly interested in computationally tractable and explainable logical reasoning, from a theoretic and a practical perspective.
