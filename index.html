Christoph Schwering

I'm a software engineer at Google in Munich. Before that, I was a researcher at RWTH Aachen and UNSW Sydney.

My research was in the area of commonsense reasoning for artificial intelligence. On the theoretical side, I looked for ways of keeping reasoning in expressive logical languages tractable. On the practical side, I worked on algorithms for reasoning in such languages and on their implementation, Limbo.
